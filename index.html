Talk about the important

How often did you think that this world is unreal? As if all this traced reality is just tinsel, hiding other, more important things. Think logically how many non-matches both in our history and in our nature. Dozens of false facts are so ingrained in human nature that no one even thinks to ask questions. “This is an obvious fact” - The standard response of the herd man. One has only to think, grabbing a subtle thought, as a clump of various facts falls on your head. "What for?" -You ask. The mere thought of possible freedom pushed people to rebellion, and imagine what would happen if a modern person finds out something superfluous. The familiar feeling of security and comfort in concrete boxes sinks into oblivion. Are we ready for the truth?
